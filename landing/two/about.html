our story

At BizPayPro, we boast a team with over 60 years of combined expertise in entrepreneurship, accounting, customer service, and software engineering. In 2023, our processing payment partner exceeded expectations by settling over $46 billion for more than 40,000 merchants, with over half of that volume qualifying for Level II & III discounts. As trusted B2B payment experts, we are dedicated to assisting merchants in constructing robust and dependable payment solutions. Backed by our 5-star client service team and personalized services, we provide tailored solutions to meet your specific business needs. Through direct partnerships with leading payment providers and a focus on business systems and strategic alliances, we empower businesses to enhance cash flow, streamline payment processes with our back-office tools, and explore diverse payment acceptance options.
